Elder Care Planning / Estate Planning

a group of qualified, experienced Elder Care Planning professionals in Maryland who are devoted to preserving and protecting assets against the rising costs of nursing home and long-term care expenses. The Medical Assistance application process is exceedingly time consuming and very complex thus our main priority is to offer families the opportunity to preserve as much of their assets as allowed by law and to present Medicaid with a complete and accurate application. Our goal on each one of our cases is to provide a positive outcome for the family.

Our Line of Elder Care Services:

  • Medicaid Eligibility
  • Medicaid Planning for Long Term care
  • Asset Protection and Preservation
  • Asset Management
  • Client Bill Paying Services
  • Consulting Services
  • Power of Attorney Documents
  • Guardianships

We understand the emotional toll that a family takes on when a loved one is admitted to a long-term care facility and how hard it is to carry on with a baggage of responsibilities. Angels of Elder Care Planning can ease those burdens and allow the family to concentrate on what should matter most, time spent with the family.
